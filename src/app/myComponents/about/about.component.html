About This Angular App

A todo app, short for "to-do" app, is a software application designed to help users organize and manage their tasks, activities, and reminders. It is a digital counterpart of the traditional pen-and-paper to-do list, providing a more flexible and convenient way to keep track of tasks and responsibilities. Typically, a todo app allows users to create lists of tasks, add details and due dates to each task, and mark tasks as completed when they are finished
